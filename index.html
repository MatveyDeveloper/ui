mastering liquid glass

To achieve the perfect "Liquid" look, use stacked <section> tags. Redow.css will automatically apply multi-layered blur and purple glow to each container.

pro tip:

Use "glass-card" class for extra refraction effects on dark backgrounds.

luxury minimal guide

Gold.css focuses on typography and warmth. It works best with serif fonts and large white spaces between sections. Ideal for high-end landing pages.

pro tip:

Combine with "text-gold" class to apply metallic gradients to your headlines.

cyber-tech deployment

The Cyber core is high-contrast. It replaces smooth curves with sharp angles and animated scanners. Best used for dashboards and dev-tools.

pro tip:

Add the "glitch" attribute to your buttons for an authentic cyberpunk hover effect.
